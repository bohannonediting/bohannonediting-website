Bohannon Editing

Steve Bohannon provides copy editing, consulting, and research services for authors, researchers, journalists, marketers, and business professionals. For more than a decade, Steve has worked with writers from all walks of life. For example, last year he edited social media and blog content for one of America’s largest public charter school management companies - National Heritage Academies. He’s also recently edited content for Christianity Today, one of the most prestigious religious periodicals in North America.

For a personalized review of your editing needs, please contact Steve directly.
